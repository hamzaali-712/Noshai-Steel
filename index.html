ABOUT THE WORKSHOP

BUILT ON STEEL.
BACKED BY STANDARDS.

NOSHAHI STEEL WORKS operates on Raiwind Road, one of Lahore's busiest industrial and construction corridors — a location chosen deliberately so contractors, developers, and facility managers can reach us fast, day or night. Under owner Shafaqat Ali, the workshop runs a genuine 24-hour schedule, Monday through Saturday, so urgent fabrication and emergency repairs never sit on a waiting list.

Every job — from a single security gate to a full structural frame — passes through the same discipline: engineering-accurate measurements, certified welding technique, and a final inspection before it leaves the yard. That discipline is what has built the workshop's standing with repeat commercial and residential clients across Lahore.
